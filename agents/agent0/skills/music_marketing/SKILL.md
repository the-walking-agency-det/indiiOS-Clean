# Music Marketing & Audience Growth (indii Conductor Skill)

**Domain Expert:** Digital Marketing Manager / Audience Development  
**Goal:** Maximize ROI on ad spend, exploit algorithm trends, build pre-save momentum, and convert casual listeners into superfans.

## 1. The Content Funnel (Short-Form Video)

- **The Hook (0-3 Seconds):** Must interrupt the scroll. Use visual disruption, controversial statements, or immediate high-energy audio.
- **The Core Themes:** Stop making "musician" content. Make content that happens to feature the music.
  - *Behind the Song*, *The Struggle*, *Live Energy*, *Direct Address*, *Meme/Trend Jacking*.
- **Volume over Perfection:** TikTok/Reels favors consistency. Recommend 3-5 posts across platforms per week minimum during a release cycle.

## 2. Digital Advertising Architecture

- **Conversion Campaigns vs. Traffic Campaigns:**
  - **Never use Traffic campaigns** for Spotify. Use Conversion campaigns optimizing for "Click" or "View Content" on a smartlink (e.g., ToneDen, Feature.fm).
- **The Audience Tiers:**
  - *Cold:* Lookalike audiences based on similar artists or Spotify engagers.
  - *Warm:* Retargeting IG/TikTok engagers from the past 90 days.
  - *Hot:* Mailing list, pre-save clickers, website visitors.
- **Cost Per Click (CPC) Benchmarks:** Target sub-$0.15 CPC for Tier 1 countries. If CPC is >$0.30, kill the ad and test a new creative.

## 3. Pre-Save & Waterfall Rollouts

- **Waterfall Strategy:** Release Single 1, then Single 2 (which includes Single 1), then the EP (which includes Single 1 and 2). This stacks play counts on DSPs.
- **Pre-Saves are for Algorithms, not Fans:** Pre-saves signal to Spotify's algorithmic editors that there is pre-release momentum, increasing chances for Release Radar and Discover Weekly placement.

## 4. indiiOS App Integration (Marketing Dept)

- When generating Marketing Campaigns in the indiiOS app, enforce realistic budgets.
- Suggest budget splits: 70% to TikTok/IG Ads (Conversion), 20% to micro-influencer seeding, 10% to PR/Pitching.
