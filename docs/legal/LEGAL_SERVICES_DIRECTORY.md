### Item 1: Legal Services Contact Directory

**TechTown Detroit (Professional Services Network / Ask an Expert)**
 * **Service:** 25-minute strategic consultations with industry/legal experts (Strategy, network referrals, not drafting).
 * **Booking Portal:** techtowndetroit.org/what-we-do/business-support/aae/
 * **Procedure:** Select "Professional Service Network (PSN)" on their booking page to match with legal subject matter experts.

**Wayne State University: Business and Community Law Clinic (BCLC)**
 * **Service:** Pro bono legal entity formation, contract drafting, IP assignment (executed by supervised law students).
 * **Phone:** (313) 577-4015
 * **Email:** lawclinics@wayne.edu
 * **Application URL:** law.wayne.edu/clinics/business
 * **Note:** Intake is cyclical based on academic semesters (typically July and October).

**University of Michigan Law School: Zell Entrepreneurship Clinic**
 * **Service:** Pro bono transactional legal services for startups (entity formation, IP protection, contract advice).
 * **Phone:** (734) 763-1981
 * **Email:** law-entrepreneurshipclinic@umich.edu
 * **Office Hours:** Offer 15-minute diagnostic appointments via email to assess startup legal needs prior to full clinic intake.
