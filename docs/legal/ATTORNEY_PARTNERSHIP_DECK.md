## What indiiOS Is

indiiOS is the all-in-one creative platform built specifically for
independent music producers, visual artists, and creators. We handle:

- **AI-powered music & image creation** — generate, edit, and export
  professional creative assets
- **Music distribution** — deliver directly to Spotify, Apple Music,
  Tidal, and every major DSP
- **Business operations** — split sheets, royalty tracking, contracts,
  copyright registration, and more

We are in beta, launching publicly in 2026, and building the platform
that independent artists have always needed — one that treats them like
the business owners they are.

## Why Legal Is Central to What We Do

Every indiiOS user eventually hits a legal moment:

| User action | Legal need |
|---|---|
| Releases their first record | Distribution agreement, rights clearance |
| Collaborates with another artist | Split sheet, co-writer agreement |
| Gets a sync placement offer | Sync license review |
| Samples another song | Mechanical license, sample clearance |
| Signs with a label or publisher | Contract review, negotiation |
| Uses AI to generate music or art | Copyright status, ownership declaration |
| Files for copyright registration | Guided filing with the Copyright Office |

We have built a **Legal module** directly into the platform with
contract analysis, DMCA tools, and an attorney directory. When users hit
these moments, we want them to find you — immediately, in context,
without leaving the app.

## The Deal Structure

We are flexible. Three models to choose from:

| Model | Structure | Best if |
|---|---|---|
| **Revenue Share** | indiiOS receives a percentage of client fees from referred users. You get placement at zero upfront cost. | You want no financial risk while the platform grows |
| **Flat Retainer** | Monthly fee for the featured slot. You keep 100% of client fees. | Client volume is high enough to justify a fixed cost |
| **Hybrid** *(recommended)* | Low/zero monthly fee at launch + revenue share after a mutually agreed threshold. You hold the "Founding Partner Attorney" title and co-marketing rights. | Both parties want skin in the game from day one |

We are open to structuring this any way that works for your firm.
The goal is a long-term relationship, not a one-off ad buy.
