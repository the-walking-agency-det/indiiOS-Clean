# indii Growth Protocol: The Ultimate Meta-to-Spotify Pipeline

## Overview

The **indii Growth Protocol** acts as your autonomous digital marketing agency. Native to **indiiOS**, it connects your creative assets to Meta's advertising ecosystem (specifically Instagram) to generate a targeted, algorithmic surge on Spotify.

Instead of burning hours and budget trying to manually optimize ads, you set your budget, select a timeline template, and let the **indii Conductor** handle the rest.

---

## How It Works

### 1. The Meta Andromeda Pipeline (Creative Generation)

When you trigger the Growth Protocol, the system doesn't rely on just one or two ad variations. Using the **Meta Andromeda** pipeline, the indii Conductor analyzes your release metadata, cover art, and short-form video assets, and automatically generates up to **15 different creative variations**.

This includes different hooks, video cuts, and copy treatments to ensure the algorithm has enough data to find the cheapest, most engaged audience.

### 2. Strict Purity: The "Instagram-Only" Matrix

Standard advertising platforms want to spend your money on low-quality desktop sidebars or off-platform "Audience Networks" that yield cheap clicks but zero actual streaming value.

The indii Growth Protocol enforces a strict **Instagram-Only** placement matrix:

- **Instagram Stories**
- **Instagram Reels**
- **Instagram Feed**

This ensures every cent of your budget is spent in mobile-first environments where users are already primed for music discovery.

### 3. The 28-Day Front-Loaded Algorithmic Spike

The primary objective of the protocol is not just to acquire streams, but to manipulate the **Spotify Algorithm** into giving you organic reach via *Discover Weekly* and *Release Radar*.

By selecting the `indii_28_day_frontloaded` timeline template, the system schedules your campaign to spend specifically heavy in the initial days following a release. This aggressive, unnatural spike in saves and listeners signals to Spotify that a track is going viral, triggering organic algorithmic playlisting.

### 4. The Autonomous Kill-Switch (Cost-Per-Save Control)

You do not need to constantly monitor your ad manager to stop bleeding money. The indii Conductor actively watches your campaigns and optimizes for **Cost-Per-Save (CPS)**.

- **The Safety Floor:** The protocol expects a minimum 5% conversion rate.
- **The Kill-Switch:** If any of the 15 creative variations fail to convert efficiently or cost too much per save, the system’s `ViralScoreService` acts as a kill-switch. It instantly pauses underperforming ads and funnels remaining budget into the proven winners automatically.

### 5. Transitioning to Curator Playlists

As your ad campaign runs, the `PlatformDataService` tracks your **Spotify Popularity Score**. Once your track hits algorithmic milestones (e.g., passing a Score of 30), the protocol alerts you.

At this stage, your track has proven market validity. You can then invoke the `indii_curator_playlist_builder` template, which pivots the strategy to directly target and pitch independent third-party playlist curators, extending the lifespan and consistent streaming numbers of your release.

## Instructions for Use

1. **Navigate to Marketing:** Open the **Marketing Dashboard** in the indiiOS right-panel sidebar.
2. **Select Asset:** Choose the track/release you want to promote and ensure your audio/video assets are loaded in the Creative Studio.
3. **Choose the Growth Template:** From the Campaign Timeline dropdown, select **"indii Growth Protocol: 28-Day Front-Loaded"**.
4. **Approve Budget:** Assign your total campaign budget.
5. **Launch:** Click **Deploy Protocol**. The *indii Conductor* assumes control.

### Advanced Settings

While the system is entirely autonomous, founders and advanced users can view real-time CPS tracking, Popularity Score deltas, and view which creative variants (under the Meta Andromeda test) won the A/B testing sprint directly from the Analytics tab.
