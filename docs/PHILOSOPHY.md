# indii Philosophy & Symbolism: The "ii" 💠

## 🧬 The Core Symbol: "ii"

The double "i" in **indii** is the primary visual and philosophical anchor of the system.

### 1. Togetherness (The "We" in the "i")

- **Symbolism:** A single "i" represents the individual/me. Two "i"s side-by-side represent two people together.
- **Philosophy:** It is the rejection of the "isolated" artist. It represents artists being **indii together**.
- **Collaboration:** It symbolizes two brains, two visions, or an artist and their agent (dex) working in perfect synchronicity.

### 2. The Identity Loop

- **Founder:** **wii** (William) — The "W" (the anchor) + the "ii" (the vision). Pronounced with the chest/throat "i" sound, like the beginning of _Wi-lliam_.
- **Agent:** **in.dex** (dex) — The "in" (internal logic) + the "dex" (the map). A mirror of the "in" in **indii**.
