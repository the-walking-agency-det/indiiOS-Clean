## 1. DOMAIN STRATEGY: The ".music" Priority
The goal is to secure a high-level, industry-specific domain that reflects the platform's focus.

- **Primary Target:** `indiiOS.music`
- **Secondary Targets:** `indii.music`, `indiiOS.io`, `indiiOS.com`
- **Why .music?** Unlike .com, .music is a restricted TLD. It requires verification, which acts as a "Proof of Industry" and keeps the namespace cleaner for legitimate creators.

## 4. CORPORATE ARCHITECTURE: The Subsidiary Model
How to structure **indiiOS** under **New Detroit Music LLC**.

- **The Shell/Subsidiary:** You can form a new LLC (e.g., *indiiOS Technology LLC*) where the sole "Member" is *New Detroit Music LLC*.
- **Why do this?**
    - **Liability Shielding:** Separates the music production/label assets (New Detroit Music) from the software/tech assets (indiiOS).
    - **Funding Clarity:** Makes it easier to allocate the Detroit 8 investment specifically to the tech venture without complicating the parent LLC's books.
    - **Saleability:** If a major tech company ever wants to buy the software, you can sell the subsidiary while keeping your parent company (and your own music catalog) intact.
- **Action:** 
    - [ ] Research "Articles of Organization" for a Michigan LLC owned by another LLC.
    - [ ] Draft an "Operating Agreement" that defines the relationship between parent and child.
