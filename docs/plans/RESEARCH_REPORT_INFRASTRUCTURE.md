# RESEARCH REPORT: INFRASTRUCTURE & LEGAL (2026-02-17)

## 1. Michigan LLC Subsidiary Structure
To have **New Detroit Music LLC** own **indiiOS**, you create what is known as a **Member-Managed Subsidiary**.

- **Process:** You file new "Articles of Organization" with the State of Michigan.
- **Member:** Instead of listing "William Paul Roberts" as the individual owner, you list "New Detroit Music LLC" as the 100% owner/member.
- **Operating Agreement:** This is the most important part. It defines that New Detroit Music LLC provides the capital and oversight, while indiiOS (the subsidiary) owns the specific software IP and handles the tech operations.
- **Benefit:** If there is a legal issue with the software, it doesn't automatically touch the assets (like your masters or property) owned by the parent company.

## 3. Domain: .music
- **Availability:** `indiiOS.music` is likely available as it's a very specific name.
- **Registration:** Most registrars (Namecheap, etc.) handle .music now, but you must provide a "Music Community Member" ID or verify your status as a creator/label during the process.
- **Strategic Note:** The `.music` extension is the most "sovereign" choice for a platform like ours. It separates us from the generic tech world (.io / .com) and places us firmly in the industry hierarchy.
