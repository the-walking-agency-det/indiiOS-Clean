ARTIST NOTES - KingVerse
========================

Real Name: Marcus "Verse" Thompson
Stage Name: KingVerse  
Hometown: Detroit, MI (Eastside, born & raised)
Age: 23
Label: Independent (self-released)

ABOUT ME:
I started making music in my grandma's basement when I was 14. She had this old Yamaha keyboard and I used to record on my phone. My sound is a mix of Detroit hip-hop grit with melodic hooks — people compare me to Tee Grizzley meets PartyNextDoor but I'm really doing my own thing.

INFLUENCES:
- J Dilla (production style, sampling philosophy)  
- Eminem (lyricism, storytelling)
- Big Sean (Detroit pride, melody)
- PartyNextDoor (R&B vibe, mood)
- Sada Baby (raw energy, Detroit slang)

BRAND VIBE:
Dark, cinematic, Detroit grit. Think midnight blue and gold. I want my brand to feel like a movie — every release is a chapter. My logo is a crown with a microphone woven into it (I have a rough sketch).
