# 🌐 Music 4.0: The Sovereign Roadmap
## From Rental to Ownership

### Phase 1: The Sovereign Foundation (Current)
*   **The Vault:** Local-first encryption for masters. Spectral forensics to ensure authenticity before any upload.
*   **Direct-to-DSP (DDEX 4.3):** Eliminating the aggregator (DistroKid/Symphonic). Peer-to-Spotify/Apple delivery.
*   **The Velocity Engine:** "Studio-to-Store in Hours." By automating metadata (DDEX), cover art, and validation through specialized AI Agents, indii reduces the multi-day "waiting period" to a single session. When an artist makes fire, it hits Beatport, Spotify, and the Sound Locker while the heat is fresh.
*   **Catalog Ingestion:** A "Digital Homecoming" tool that pulls existing back-catalogs from legacy aggregators and DSPs into the indii Vault. This allows labels to manage 100% of their history in one sovereign interface.
*   **Smart Splits:** On-chain revenue waterfalls. Instant, transparent payouts to collaborators (ISRC-linked).

### Phase 2: The Sound Locker (Fan-to-Artist)
*   **Social Architecture:** A clean, MySpace-inspired communication hub. No "feed" algorithms—just direct connection.
*   **Digital/Physical Artifacts:** Selling digital collectibles (stems, exclusive art) and physical goods directly from the Locker.
*   **Premium Streetwear Integration:** Partnering with high-end providers like **Tapstitch** to allow artists to design and drop premium streetwear (hoodies, tees, artifacts) with zero inventory risk.
*   **Direct Printing:** Future roadmap includes acquiring/building local Detroit direct-to-garment and 3D printing hubs.

### Phase 3: The Licensing Engine (The Marketplace)
*   **Zero-Commission Licensing:** Artists set their price for film/TV/Commercial use. 
*   **Platform Fee Model:** We charge a flat fee ($50) to the licensee; the artist keeps 100% of their requested license fee.
*   **Automated Clearance:** Smart contracts handle the license grant and the split payout in one transaction.

### The Movement Milestone (Memorial Day 2026)
*   **The Sovereign Stage:** Partnering with Pax (Paxahau) to provide indii as the official artist infrastructure for Movement.
*   **The "Velocity Stunt":** A high-profile artist creates a track in the studio on Saturday and releases it through the indii Velocity Engine by Sunday morning. 
    *   **Level 1 (Instant):** Global release to the artist's **Sound Locker** for direct fan support.
    *   **Level 2 (Fast-Track):** Direct DDEX delivery to Beatport and Spotify via the indii bypass.
*   **Genesis Node Onboarding:** Onboarding the first "Detroit 8" founders live during the festival.
