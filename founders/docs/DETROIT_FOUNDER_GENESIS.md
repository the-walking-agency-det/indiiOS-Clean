### The Architecture of Independence

#### 1. The Vault (Local-First Security)
*   **The Concept:** Your music never leaves your hardware until it is ready for the world.
*   **The Tech:** Military-grade encryption and local spectral forensics. We fingerprint your music locally to ensure its "Chain of Custody" is unbroken before it hits the network.

#### 2. Peer-to-DSP (Zero Middlemen)
*   **The Concept:** No more DistroKid. No more Symphonic. No more monthly rent for your own career.
*   **The Tech:** Direct DDEX/ERN 4.3 integration. indii speaks the language of Spotify, Apple, and Tidal directly. You press "Submit," and the code handles the rest. You keep 100% of the revenue.

#### 3. The Trust Protocol (Smart Splits)
*   **The Concept:** Pay your team instantly, transparently, and forever.
*   **The Tech:** Automated revenue waterfalls on the blockchain. When a cent comes in, it is split according to the digital contract you signed with your collaborators. No math, no arguments.

#### 4. AI for Exploitation, Not Creation
*   **The Concept:** AI should work *for* the artist, not replace them.
*   **The Tech:** Automated marketing assets, metadata optimization, and distribution strategy. indii takes the fear out of AI by using it to protect and project your original sound.
