# Social & Audience Module (RC1)

The Social module integrates directly with the artist's digital presence across platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and Twitter. It serves as an audience engagement and content distribution hub, bridging the gap between creative assets and community growth.

## 📱 Key Features
- **Platform Sync:** Automated cross-posting and content scheduling for visual and audio assets.
- **Engagement Analytics:** Real-time tracking of follower growth, engagement rates, and content performance.
- **Social Dashboard:** A unified view of all connected accounts and pending posts.
- **Content Automation:** Automated generation of platform-specific captions and hashtags.
- **Audience Insights:** AI-driven analysis of fan demographics and sentiment.

## 🏗️ Technical Architecture
- **`SocialService`**: Core logic for managing third-party platform integrations and API tokens.
- **`SocialDashboard`**: Central UI for artists and social media managers.
- **Zod Schemas**: Strict validation for social post metadata and account configurations.
- **Marketing Agent Integration**: Automated creation of social content briefs and aesthetic consistency checks.

## 🔗 Integrations
- **Creative Studio:** Direct one-click sharing of generated images and posters.
- **Video Studio:** Automated export of formatted reels, shorts, and TikToks.
- **Marketing:** Direct link between social performance and overall campaign ROI.
